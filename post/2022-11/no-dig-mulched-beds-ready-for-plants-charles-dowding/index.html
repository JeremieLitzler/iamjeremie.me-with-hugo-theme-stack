No dig gardening, you can save a lot of time!

Nov 15, 2022
1 minute read

Thanks to Charles Dowding for sharing his wisdom and knowledge! I wrote the following notes watching the video published on Charles Dowding’s channel. You can watch it using this YouTube link.

In this very short vlog, the two lessons are:

  • when you get the compost before the new year, the compost will become smooth, ready to be sown in the spring.
  • using a rake, you remove any weed seeds that may think they can have a go!

That’s it!
